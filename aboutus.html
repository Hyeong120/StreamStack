The Project

StreamStack is a creative project developed as our final requirement for Intro to Computing 1.

Our primary objective was to successfully execute and integrate the core components of a modern movie streaming website:

  • Front-End Interface: Creating an intuitive and attractive user experience.
  • Back-End Infrastructure: Managing data, user authentication, and system logic.

This platform represents the result of our learning and collaborative effort. We hope you enjoy the product of our hard work!
